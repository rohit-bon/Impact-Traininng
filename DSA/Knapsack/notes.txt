Knapsack:
    Knapsack problem is example of comnbinational optimization of problem
    it is also knnown as as ruckseek problem
    the name of the problem is defined in the form of the maximization problem
    given a bag with maximum weight capacity of w and set of itmes, each having a weight and value associated with it, Deccide the number of each item to take collection such  that total weight > capacity 
    Types of knapsack:
        1. Fractional Knapsack
        2. 0/1 knapsack
        3. Bounded Knapsack
        4. unbounded knapsack
